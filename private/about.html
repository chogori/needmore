Online Exchange is a service made available in the hope that people will exchange their coin_cards online, as well as possibly their coins, a new revolutionary cryptocurrency. In order to keep our users safe, we make sure to authenticate admin cookies using an 8-byte salt. We figure that's too many bits to brute-force.
